Webfixus started in Bangalore in 2022 with a simple observation: most business websites are broken—slow, invisible to search engines, and silent on sales. We set out to fix that. 80+ projects later, we design, build, grow, and automate for ambitious clients across 12 countries, with a 4.9/5 rating earned by launching on time.

We are a fusion of artistry, technology, and strategy—design, development, data, marketing, and AI working seamlessly as one team. Every build ships with green Core Web Vitals, honest scoping, and automation only where it earns its keep. Launch is the starting line: we measure, iterate, and craft digital ecosystems that perform, scale, and inspire.

▶ What we believe

The principles behind every project

[ Principle 01 ]

Performance over polish

A beautiful site that loads slowly is a broken site. Every build ships with green Core Web Vitals — design and speed are never a trade-off.

[ Principle 02 ]

Honest scoping

Fixed quotes before work begins, and honest advice even when it means a smaller project. Trust compounds better than invoices.

[ Principle 03 ]

AI where it earns its keep

We integrate AI where it genuinely saves money or time — never as a sticker on the box. If a simple script does the job, we’ll tell you.

[ Principle 04 ]

Launch is the starting line

We measure what happens after go-live. Analytics, SEO, and iteration are part of every engagement, not an upsell.

Fix what's broken. Build what's missing. Grow what's working. Automate what's repetitive.
